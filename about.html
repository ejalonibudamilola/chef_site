Our Vision


Debrafish Cafeteria is committed to help you order food online with less stress and also feeding the nation in hygiene and ensure acceptable food presentation and also to produce professional chef that will beat world record.

How it began...


Debrafish Cafeteria started her operation as a chef company in the year 2000. It became recognised internationally in 2004 and as since then been trusted by all for quality service in catering, home services and training of future chef. The arm of training has made a great impact in the society by producing experts in catering service. Giving teachings and orientation to the masses on food hygiene, nutrition and dietetics.
